By Year 3 you'll have: certifications, GitHub presence, shipped products with users, revenue track record, and optionally a technical article or open-source contribution. That's an O-1A profile.
Alternative: US remote contract secured before relocating → use income + offer letter to support visa application. Target US AI startups, not corporates — faster hiring, more remote-friendly.
